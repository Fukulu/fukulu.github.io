MouseKit for Philips SPK7858

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Furkan Tumay, operating as FoaltyCoder ("we", "our", or "us"), built MouseKit for Philips SPK7858 ("the App") as a commercial application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use the App.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.) is collected.
  • No usage analytics or telemetry data is collected.
  • No crash reports are sent to any server.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used.
  • No data is transmitted to any external server or third party.
  • No user accounts are required or created.

How the App Works

The App operates entirely on your device and uses the following system permissions:

  • Bluetooth: Used solely to communicate with your Philips SPK7858 mouse for battery level monitoring and connection status. Bluetooth data is processed locally and never leaves your device.
  • Accessibility: Used solely to enable mouse button remapping and scroll direction control. Input events are processed locally and never recorded or transmitted.

Data Storage

All user preferences (button mappings, selected profile, language preference, and notification settings) are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed when you uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.
